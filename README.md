# GoeLore

A digital platform designed to preserve, teach, and reconnect people with their cultural roots in a modern, interactive way.

## Overview

Culture is gradually being lost as fewer traditions, languages, and stories are passed down through generations. This platform addresses that problem by providing a centralized space where users can learn about their heritage, interact with others from similar backgrounds, and contribute cultural knowledge.

The system combines education, artificial intelligence, and community networking to create a living digital archive of culture.

---

## Features

### User Profiles
- Register and log in
- Select or identify with a cultural background
- Track learning progress

### Cultural Archive
- Explore structured cultural content:
  - History
  - Language and dialects
  - Food and traditions
  - Festivals and beliefs
- Search and bookmark content

### AI Cultural Tutor
- Chat-based learning experience
- Ask questions about traditions, language, and history
- Personalized responses based on user culture

### Community Features
- Connect with people from the same culture
- Join discussions and share experiences
- Discover or create cultural events

### Contributions
- Users can submit cultural knowledge
- Support for text, images, and audio
- Moderation system for accuracy

### Monetization (Optional)
- Paid language courses
- Premium cultural content

## Problem Statement

Many people today are disconnected from their cultural roots due to modernization and lack of knowledge transfer between generations. Cultural information is often scattered and difficult to access, leading to the gradual loss of traditions and languages.
